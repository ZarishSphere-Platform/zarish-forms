# Zarish Forms Governance

Zarish Forms enforces **rigorous governance** across all forms and modules.

## Rules
1. One spreadsheet = one form
2. All schemas generated from spreadsheets
3. Previews mandatory before merge
4. Approval workflows required
5. Versioned according to MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH

## Roles & Responsibilities
- **NGO Contributor** – fills spreadsheets
- **Platform Reviewer** – validates previews
- **Maintainer** – approves PRs, manages versioning

## Compliance
- FHIR R4 mapping for interoperability
- Audit logs preserved
- Governance ensures donor & regulatory alignment
